Michael Hobley, Ph.D.
Postdoctoral Reasearcher @ CalTech
ML Product Owner @ Noonien

I am currently a postdoctoral researcher at CalTech working in AI for ecology. I am also the founder of Noonien, a Machine Learning consultancy engineering custom solutions for businesses and indivuals.
I am a recent DPhil graduate in Computer Vision and Machine Learning from the Active Vision Laboratory group at the Engineering Science department of the University of Oxford.
My research interests and experience are broad, spanning: ecology, generative AI, LLMs, weakly-supervise computer vision, large-scale data understanding, and engineering pipeline acceleration.
