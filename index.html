Michael Hobley, Ph.D.
Postdoctoral Researcher @ Caltech
ML Product Owner @ Noonien

I am currently a postdoctoral researcher in the Perona Lab at Caltech working in AI for ecology. I am also the founder of Noonien, a Machine Learning consultancy engineering custom solutions for businesses and individuals.
I am a recent DPhil graduate in Computer Vision and Machine Learning from the Active Vision Laboratory group at the Engineering Science department of the University of Oxford.
My research interests and experience are broad, spanning: computer vision for ecology, weakly-supervised computer vision, active acoustics, low-SNR vision, large-scale data understanding, and engineering pipeline acceleration.
